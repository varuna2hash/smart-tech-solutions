About the Founder

Varuna Priyanka

I am a passionate Robotics Educator and Engineer specializing in Robotics, 3D Printing, Embedded Systems, and Web Application Development.

With hands-on experience in designing intelligent mobile robots, motor control systems, IoT-based solutions, and energy harvesting technologies, my mission is to empower the next generation of innovators.

At Fast Track Robotics, we provide structured learning programs that guide students from the fundamentals of electronics and coding to advanced AI-integrated robotic systems.

I strongly believe that practical learning, creativity, and real-world project development are the keys to building future engineers and tech leaders.
